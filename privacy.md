# Privacy

Tools and services to allow privacy on web browsing as also as on your local machine.

- [Browser Tor](https://www.torproject.org)
- [DNS - 1.1.1.1](https://one.one.one.one)
- [Portable operating system - Tail](https://tails.net)
- [Application firewall - OpenSnitch](https://github.com/evilsocket/opensnitch)
- [Search engine - DuckDuckGo](https://duckduckgo.com)
- [Browser extension to block trackers - Privacy Badger](https://privacybadger.org)
- [Browswer extension to block Ads - AdBlock](https://getadblock.com)
- [Firefox Relay - protect your email](https://relay.firefox.com/)
